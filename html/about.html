Our Story

Founded in 2010, SkyWay Airlines has grown to become one of India's most trusted and loved airlines. With a fleet of over 100 aircraft and 1000+ daily flights, we connect 100+ destinations across India and internationally.

Our commitment to punctuality, affordability, and exceptional service has earned us the loyalty of millions of passengers. We believe in making air travel accessible to everyone without compromising on quality and safety.
