Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SkyWay Airlines

How do I book a flight on SkyWay?

You can book a flight easily through our website or mobile app. Simply enter your travel details (from, to, date, passengers), select your preferred flight, enter passenger details, choose seats, add extras, make payment, and receive your confirmation via email/SMS.

What is the baggage allowance?

Economy Class: 15kg check-in + 7kg cabin baggage. Business Class: 25kg check-in + 10kg cabin baggage. Infants: 10kg check-in. Additional baggage can be purchased online for discounted rates.

How can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking through 'My Bookings' section on our website or app. Cancellation charges apply based on how close to departure you cancel. Full refund available for cancellations made 24 hours before departure.

Can I select my seat in advance?

Yes! You can select your seat during the booking process. We offer window, aisle, and middle seats. Preferred seats may have an additional charge.

What documents do I need for domestic travel?

For domestic travel, you need any government-issued ID proof: Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License, or PAN Card. For international travel, valid passport and visa are required.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. Check-in counters close 60 minutes before departure.
